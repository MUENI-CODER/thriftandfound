Our Story

Thrift & Found was born from a simple belief: fashion shouldn't cost the earth. We curate high-quality secondhand clothing that tells a story.

Sustainable Style

Every item bought secondhand saves approximately 2.5kg of CO2, 2,700 liters of water, and reduces textile waste. We're proud to be part of the circular fashion movement.

Curated With Care

We hand-pick every piece for quality, style, and uniqueness. From vintage band tees to designer denim, we ensure only the best makes it to our racks.

Community First

We're more than a shop — we're a gathering place for thrift lovers, vintage enthusiasts, and anyone who loves a good find and sustainable living.
